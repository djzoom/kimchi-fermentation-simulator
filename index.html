How to read this simulator

You do not need a lab background. Read the chart in three simple passes.

1

Find the best eating window

Read the red score line and the green dashed marker first. That is your fastest route to “when should I eat this batch?”.

2

Check safety before enthusiasm

The nitrite line tells you when very early fermentation is still not ideal. Flavor and safety should be read together.

3

Use the microbe story to understand taste

The phase bands and microbe cards explain why kimchi tastes fresh, sparkly, or deep and sour at different points.
